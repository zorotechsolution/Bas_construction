About BAS

Home/About
The Studio

An Atelier of
Architecture & Craft

Founded on the belief that great architecture is born from disciplined imagination, BAS Baby Architects Studio is a multidisciplinary practice operating at the intersection of design, engineering and craft. We bring together architects, interior designers, 3D visualisers and construction managers under one creative roof — ensuring a single, accountable team from concept to completion.

Our work spans private residences, hospitality, retail and commercial landmarks. Each project, regardless of scale, is treated as a unique composition of site, light, material and human ritual. We listen first, design second, and build only when the vision is fully resolved.

Over the years, BAS has become a trusted name for clients who value longevity over trend, clarity over clutter, and substance over surface. Our portfolio is not defined by a single style — it is defined by a single standard: excellence.

Our Mission

To craft architectural spaces that elevate daily life — combining timeless aesthetics, modern engineering and rigorous project discipline. We exist to translate our clients' aspirations into built environments that endure for generations.

Every BAS project commits to three pillars: design integrity, structural honesty and transparent delivery. We measure our success not by accolades but by the long-term satisfaction of those who inhabit our spaces.

Our Vision

To be South Asia's most trusted luxury architecture studio — recognised for design excellence, technical innovation and a culture of craftsmanship that redefines what clients expect from their architect.

We envision a future where every BAS building becomes a quiet landmark in its neighbourhood: a place people return to, photograph, and remember. Architecture that ages gracefully and serves its purpose for decades.

Design Philosophy

Principles That Shape
Every BAS Project

Our philosophy is not a style — it is a discipline. These six principles guide every decision, from the first sketch to the final handover.

Light First

We design with the sun, not against it. Orientation, aperture and shadow are composed before any line is drawn on plan.

Material Honesty

Stone is stone, timber is timber. We let materials speak truthfully — celebrating weight, grain, texture and patina.

Human Scale

Proportion is felt before it is understood. Every room, corridor and threshold is calibrated to the body and the breath.

Quiet Sustainability

Passive cooling, local materials, rainwater harvesting — sustainability woven in silently, not signposted loudly.

Detail Devotion

God lives in the junction, the skirting, the threshold. We draw what others leave to chance — and supervise it on site.

Timeless Over Trendy

We resist the fashion cycle. A BAS building should look as composed in twenty years as it does on handover day.
