How We Work

The BAS Process

A clear, stage-gated journey that protects your budget, your timeline and your design intent — from first conversation to final handover.

01

Discovery

We listen. Brief, site, budget, lifestyle, aspirations — captured in a written design intent document before any drawing begins.

02

Concept

Sketches, mood boards, massing studies and 3D views. We explore two or three directions and converge on one with you.

03

Documentation

Detailed drawings, BOQ, specifications, statutory approvals and tender — every line and material resolved before site work begins.

04

Build & Handover

Site supervision, weekly reports, snagging and a polished handover — followed by 24 months of post-completion care.
