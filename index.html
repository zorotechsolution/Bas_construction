The Design Atelier

The Material Lab
& Physical Workshop

"At BAS, design is tactile. We craft spatial experiences where raw materials meet refined engineering."

We believe that luxury architecture is felt before it is seen. In our in-house Material Lab, we curate, test, and assemble textures, finishes, and weights to compose custom palette boards for every project — matching local stone, hand-selected timber, brushed metals, and bespoke textiles.

Adjacent to our lab is the physical workshop, where our craftspeople build detailed 3D scale models of our architectural plans. This spatial prototyping process allows our clients to see, feel, and explore their future spaces in real dimensions, resolving design complexities before construction begins.
